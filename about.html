About Our Gurudwara

Short History

Established in 1998 by Sikh families of Porirua, our Gurudwara began in a modest home. Today it stands as a sanctuary for all who seek peace.

Mission Statement

To share the universal message of Guru Nanak through Seva, Sangat, and Simran—uplifting every soul who walks through our doors.
